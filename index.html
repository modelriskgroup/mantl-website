Why MANTL exists

The independent that still answers the phone.

Save Your Energy

Lower your lift power. Keep your production.

Progressing cavity pumps are inherently efficient — no reciprocating mass, no counterbalance losses — and MANTL's pump-off control trims the over-pumping that wastes power. MANTL's reported field studies show a 54.2% average reduction in electricity consumption with no production losses.

Straight talk on the number. 54.2% is an average from MANTL's reported field studies, not a guarantee for every well. PCPs deliver the biggest savings on heavy, viscous, and moderate-depth wells. We'll model the conservative savings range for your wells before you commit.

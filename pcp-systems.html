PC Pumps

Progressing cavity pumps, sized to your well.

PCPs move heavy, viscous, and sand-laden fluid with steady, low-shear flow — the workhorse of artificial lift where beam pumps and ESPs struggle. The right pump is a match between your target rate, fluid, depth, and downhole conditions.

  • Engineered selectionWe size by target rate, fluid level, casing/tubing, and deviation — not a catalogue default.
  • Elastomer matched to your fluidStator elastomer chosen for your temperature, aromatics, H₂S and CO₂ — the single biggest driver of run life.
  • Built for run lifeDesigned to keep the pump in the ground longer and the workover truck parked.
PCPs are strongest on heavy oil, high-viscosity, sand-cut, dewatering and moderate-depth wells. For high-temperature, high-aromatic, very deep or high-GOR applications we'll tell you straight whether a PCP is the right call.

Drive Systems & Power Units

Surface power, matched to the pump.

The drive head and power unit are where efficiency and backspin safety are won or lost. MANTL builds drive systems and power units in our Alberta shops and matches them to the downhole pump so the whole string runs efficiently and safely.

  • Drive headsTorque and braking matched to the application for safe, controlled operation.
  • Power unitsBuilt in-house and serviced from stores near your leases.
  • Backspin controlManaged at the surface to protect equipment and crews.

PCP Automation

Controls engineered with the iron.

Because we build the pumps and drives, our automation isn't bolted on afterward — it's designed alongside the system. The result is tighter optimization, fewer failures, and the pump-off control that trims wasted power.

  • Pump-off controlMatch production to inflow and stop over-pumping that wastes electricity.
  • Soft-start & backspin protectionProtect the rod string and surface equipment on every cycle.
  • SCADA-readyBuilt to integrate with the host systems your production team already runs. Confirm supported protocols

Rod Solutions

Continuous rod, conventional rod, re-manufactured.

MANTL passed one million metres of continuous rod sold in Canada in 2022, and added dedicated sucker rod and guide-installation capability in Lloydminster. We run rod the way the well needs it — and recycle it responsibly.

Continuous (Co-rod)

Coiled, connection-free rod that reduces couplings and the side-loading wear that plagues deviated and horizontal wells.

Conventional rod

Proven sucker rod strings, sized and run for the application, with guide installation services.

Re-manufacturing

We re-manufacture sucker rod — lowering cost and cutting the environmental footprint of every string.

Backed by a fleet of rod transfer units working across Western Canada.
